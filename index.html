A B O U T

Katie Jane LaBauve received her undergraduate degree in Art History from the American University of Rome and is currently pursing her MBA at Emory’s Goizueta Business School. Inspired by abstract expressionist and color field painting, her photographs are studies in color and composition that encourage viewers to reconsider the photographic medium. Her work is present in numerous private and public collections.
Katie Jane currently lives in Atlanta, GA with her husband and son.
